About Us

Established in 2015, Hlophekane Engineering and General Services (Pty) Ltd is a proudly South African, black-owned company dedicated to delivering exceptional procurement consulting and supply solutions to corporate clients and government entities. Our client-focused approach drives us to provide reliable, high-quality services, including goods distribution, contractor rental, equipment leasing, and truck rental. We're committed to upholding the highest standards of professionalism, sustainability, and social responsibility.

At Hlophekane Engineering and General Services (Pty) Ltd, we integrate sustainable business practices into every aspect of our operations, ensuring a positive impact on the socio-economic landscape. Our team is dedicated to exceeding client expectations, fostering trust, and building lasting partnerships. With a foundation of sound research, feasibility studies, and optimistic outlook, we are confident in our ability to grow and succeed as a leading service provider in South Africa.

Mission

Our mission is to deliver innovative, high-quality services that empower our clients and community, while driving sustainable growth and positive impact. We are committed to leveraging our expertise to eradicate poverty, improve quality of life, and promote health and safety in the mining industry, guided by sound values and pragmatic business ethics.

Vision

We envision being South Africa's leading and most trusted service providers, renowned for our professional practices, positive impact, and responsible business ethics. We strive to be the employer of choice and a valued partner in driving growth and prosperity, built on a foundation of excellence, integrity, and sustainability.

  • Workshop Support Services (Deployment of Maintenance Team)
